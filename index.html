Projects
VoxEdge
Open Source
Repo Docs

A self-contained knowledge system that runs on a single device, speaks 50+ languages, and needs no internet. This is what "building at the edge" looks like in practice — AI that works where infrastructure doesn't.

Upload documents — PDFs, Word files, plain text — and anyone can ask questions in their own language. A school where students query textbooks they couldn't read before. A kiosk where tourists get answers in their language. A field office where workers access manuals without connectivity. VoxEdge finds the relevant passages, generates an answer, and shows exactly where it came from.

Built on Google's Gemma 4 and Cohere's Tiny Aya, swappable via model profiles. Qdrant Edge for local vector search, FastEmbed for multilingual embeddings, cross-encoder reranking for precision. Deploys as a Docker container — to a Raspberry Pi, a laptop, Fly.io, or Kubernetes.
