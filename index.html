smart systems, everywhere

There are seven billion people on this planet. Each one of them could run a business. Most don't — not because they lack the ability, but because the tools are out of reach.

The latest AI research is producing models and systems of extraordinary capability — multilingual models, autonomous agents, edge inference, document understanding. Most of it sits behind cloud APIs, enterprise pricing, and infrastructure assumptions that exclude the majority of the world.

We take what's emerging and assemble it into intelligent systems that understand business context — agents that can reason about processes, answer domain-specific questions, and act on behalf of the people they serve. Systems that work where people actually are. On devices they already own. In languages they already speak.

We believe every person is a potential business. AI agents and intelligent systems should be as accessible as a phone — pragmatic, maintainable, and built to run anywhere. Seven billion people. Seven billion potential businesses. We build the systems that help make that possible.
